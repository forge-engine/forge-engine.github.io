Why Choose Forge?

Explicit Over Magic

Write clear, predictable code without hidden behaviors. Every action in your application is intentional and traceable.

Modular Architecture

Build your application with composable modules. Use official modules or create your own to extend functionality.

Framework Agnostic

Inject your application into the framework, not the other way around. Maintain full control over your application's architecture.
